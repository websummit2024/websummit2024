What is Web Summit?

In the words of Digital Trends, we are “the stunning future of tech conferences”. According to RTÉ, Web Summit is “a star-studded, informative and inspiring event”; Politico, “the Olympics of tech”; Tech.eu, “the most important annual tech event”; and The Guardian, “Glastonbury for geeks”.
Whatever Web Summit is, it wouldn’t be possible without our incredible team of more than 300 employees, including world-class engineers, data scientists, designers, producers, marketers, salespeople, operations experts and more.
We’ve disrupted our industry by pairing mind-blowing events with unique software. We started with one: our European event, Web Summit. Now we’re making waves in North America with Collision, in Asia with RISE, and in South America with Web Summit Rio. The team is preparing to launch a host of new events over the next two years.
We’ve revolutionised how people come together. And we’re just getting started.
