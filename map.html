How do I get to Web Summit?

By metro

Oriente metro station is roughly 300 metres from the event venue (Altice Arena) and 600 metres from where you’ll register (Pavilhão de Portugal).

If you’re coming from the city, you can take the green line from Baixa-Chiado station to Alameda station. From there, you can take the red line to Oriente Station (Estação do Oriente).
